Sebrae Agro

Rural APP

Developed for small rural producers, Sebrae Agro is an app that helps them identify the best time to spray agricultural pesticides

I Developed the user experience, information architecture and user interface design

My RESPONSIBILITIES

Product Design

UI Design

Researching

I started by researching users at their workplace: the farms.

This research led me to understand how users looked for information on various services and apps to try to find the best time to spray their crops.

This app is a network of producers collaborating to share information on production data and identification of pests or diseases in the region covered by participating properties

UX STUDIES APPLIED IN THIS PROJECT
Project developed with agile methods

  • Interview with users;

  • Studies with user journey map;

  • Unstructured research;

  • A/B test;

  • Usability testing, with the high-fidelity prototype;
